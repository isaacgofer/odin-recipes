Strawberry Pie

A classic and refreshing strawberry pie with a flaky crust and sweet strawberry filling.

Ingredients

  • For the crust: 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/2 cup (1 stick) cold unsalted butter, 1/4 cup ice water
  • For the filling: 1 quart fresh strawberries, hulled and halved, 3/4 cup granulated sugar, 1/4 cup cornstarch, 1/4 cup water, 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Whipped cream for topping (optional)

Preparation

  1. For the crust: In a bowl, whisk together flour and salt. Cut in butter using a pastry blender or your fingers until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Gradually add ice water, mixing until dough comes together. Wrap in plastic wrap and chill for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). On a lightly floured surface, roll out dough to a 12-inch circle. Transfer to a 9-inch pie plate. Trim and crimp edges.
  3. For the filling: In a saucepan, combine sugar, cornstarch, and water. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture thickens and becomes clear. Remove from heat and stir in lemon juice.
  4. Add strawberries to the filling and gently mix.
  5. Pour filling into the pie crust.
  6. Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until crust is golden brown. Let cool completely before serving.
  7. Top with whipped cream, if desired.
