Condensed Milk Flan

A creamy and decadent flan made with condensed milk, a classic Brazilian dessert.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can (12 oz) evaporated milk
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • For the caramel: 1/2 cup granulated sugar, 2 tablespoons water

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. For the caramel: In a saucepan over medium heat, combine sugar and water. Cook, swirling occasionally, until sugar dissolves and turns a golden amber color. Pour caramel into a flan dish or ramekins, tilting to coat the bottom.
  3. In a blender, combine condensed milk, evaporated milk, eggs, and vanilla extract. Blend until smooth.
  4. Pour mixture into the caramel-lined dish or ramekins.
  5. Place the dish or ramekins in a larger baking pan. Fill the pan with hot water halfway up the sides of the dish or ramekins (bain-marie).
  6. Bake for 40-50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Let cool completely before inverting onto a serving plate.
