Homemade Caramelized Milk Sweet

A delicious and easy to make homemade caramelized milk sweet, perfect to eat with a spoon, with cheese or in your favorite recipes.

Ingredients

  • 1 liter of whole milk
  • 4 cups of sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 cinnamon stick (optional)

Preparation

  1. In a large pot, mix the milk, sugar and baking soda.
  2. Add the cinnamon stick, if desired.
  3. Bring the pot to medium heat, stirring constantly, until the sugar dissolves.
  4. When it starts to boil, lower the heat and continue stirring occasionally to prevent sticking to the bottom of the pot.
  5. Cook the caramelized milk sweet for about 1 hour and 30 minutes, or until it has a caramel color and a creamy consistency.
  6. Remove the cinnamon stick and let the caramelized milk sweet cool before serving.
